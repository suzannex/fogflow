Fog x Ebb x Flow

Inspiration

Over the course of several visits to different FOG x FLO installations, I found myself repeatedly drawn to how the ebb and flow of park visitors was changed, or unchanged, by the presence of the fog.

This flow was particularly fascinating to me at the FOG x Island site, where fog appears every half hour on a fairly heavily trafficked bicycle and pedestrian path. The fog sometimes drew significant attendance due to its accessible location, yet equally often, the fog was ignored by swaths of commuters, joggers, and dog walkers passing by.

Guide

I visited FOG x Island on different dates, at different times, and collected data on the people I observed passing through the park over a period of time.

I recorded each individual's time of arrival, time of departure, direction, and mode of transportation: walking, running, or cycling.

Each dot in these animations represents a single visitor, traveling along their path through the park.
